In this post, we review the 2018 stock price returns for US property and casualty (re)insurers.

The top 5 performing stocks are shown in the graph below. This group includes two Florida specialists (HCI, UVE), two heavy E&S carriers (ARGO, KINSL), and one stock impacted by M&A (NAVG). All of the companies are on the smaller side with market capitalizations under $2.5b.

If we focus on the largest five P&C stocks (by market cap), we can see that all of them except for PGR were down for the year. CB and TRV were down roughly in line with the S&P 500, which was down around 6% for 2018 (including dividends). ALL was down slightly more than the S&P 500, whereas AIG was down considerably more.

The table below summarizes the 2018 performance for all US listed P&C (re)insurer stocks.
